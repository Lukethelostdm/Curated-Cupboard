If you have any questions about this policy or your data, contact:

What We Collect

We only collect information you choose to provide via our contact form or email, such as:

We use your information to:

Legal Basis

We process your information in order to:

  • Take steps towards entering into a contract, and
  • Manage and operate our business under legitimate interests.

Your Rights
